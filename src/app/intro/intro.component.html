Open History Map Data Index

Tracking the data about our past

Since the beginning of our project, the most complex operation was the search for datasets. Conversion, uniformation and normalization is complicated, but technology helps. The search for the right dataset is, on the other hand, very complicated and requires us to think like somebody we often do not know.

This is the reason behind the OHM Data Index, switching the context to the amazing OKFN Open Data Index.
